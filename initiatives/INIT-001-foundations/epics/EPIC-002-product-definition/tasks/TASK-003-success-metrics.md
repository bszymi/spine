# TASK-003 — Success Metrics

**Epic:** EPIC-002 — Product Definition
**Initiative:** INIT-001 — Foundations
**Status:** Complete

---

## Purpose

Define measurable criteria that indicate whether Spine is achieving its intended purpose.

## Deliverable

`/product/success-metrics.md`

Content should define:

- qualitative success indicators (structural integrity, traceability, reproducibility)
- quantitative metrics where applicable
- how success is evaluated at different stages of maturity

## Acceptance Criteria

- metrics are defined and traceable to product purpose
- metrics distinguish between structural success and adoption success
- evaluation approach is documented
