# TASK-004 — Product Boundaries and Constraints

---

## Purpose

Define the structural and operational boundaries within which Spine operates, and the constraints that shape its design.

## Deliverable

`/product/boundaries-and-constraints.md`

Content should define:

- system boundaries (what Spine owns vs. delegates to external tools)
- operational constraints (Git-native, artifact-centric, governed execution)
- integration boundaries (how Spine relates to external systems)
- constitutional constraints that shape the product

## Acceptance Criteria

- boundaries between Spine and external systems are clearly drawn
- constraints derived from the Constitution are explicitly referenced
- document provides clear guidance for architecture decisions
