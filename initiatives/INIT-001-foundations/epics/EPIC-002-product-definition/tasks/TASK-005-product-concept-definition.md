# TASK-005 — Product Concept Definition

---

## Purpose

Produce a single, authoritative document that defines what Spine is as a product — the "product-to-execution system" concept made concrete.

## Deliverable

`/product/product-definition.md`

Content should define:

- what Spine is in one clear statement
- the problem it solves
- how it differs from existing tools
- the core product model (artifacts, workflows, actors)
- relationship to the Charter and Constitution

## Acceptance Criteria

- a new reader can understand what Spine is from this document alone
- the product concept is concrete, not aspirational
- document is consistent with Charter and Constitution
