# EPIC-002 — Product Definition

---

## Purpose

Clearly define what Spine is, who it is for, and what problems it solves before architecture is finalized.

Without a stable product definition, architecture becomes speculative and governance lacks grounding.

---

## Key Work Areas

- Define target users and use cases
- Define non-goals and anti-requirements
- Define success metrics
- Define product boundaries and constraints
- Clarify the "product-to-execution system" concept

---

## Primary Outputs

- Product definition documentation
- User personas and use cases
- Non-goals / anti-scope definition

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Product intent is explicit and versioned
- Target users and use cases are documented
- Non-goals are clearly stated
- Architecture work (EPIC-003) can proceed with a stable product foundation
